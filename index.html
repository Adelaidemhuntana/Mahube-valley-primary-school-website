Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At Mahube Valley Primary School, we are committed to nurturing both the academic and cultural growth of our students. Our cultural activities, including heritage celebrations, art exhibitions, music performances, and community engagement projects, are designed to enrich the educational experience. These events allow students to express their creativity, explore diverse cultures, and develop a strong sense of community.

Through these activities, we aim to foster well-rounded individuals who appreciate their heritage and understand the importance of cultural diversity. Join us in celebrating the vibrant spirit of our school!

School Behavior Expectations

At Mahube Valley Primary School, we prioritize a safe and respectful learning environment. It is important for all students to adhere to the following behavioral guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

No Bullying

Bullying of any kind will not be tolerated. Treat everyone with respect and kindness.

No Disruption

Disrupting classes and interfering with the learning process of others is prohibited.

Respect Property

Students are expected to respect school property and the belongings of others.

Follow Instructions

All students must follow the instructions of teachers and staff promptly and respectfully.

No Cheating

Academic honesty is crucial; cheating and plagiarism are strictly prohibited.

Be Safe

Safety is our top priority; students should avoid any behavior that could endanger themselves or others.

Our Heads of Departments

At Mahube Valley Primary School, our Heads of Departments (HODs) are highly skilled professionals who drive academic excellence and foster a culture of innovation.

Each HOD brings a wealth of experience, deep subject-matter expertise, and a passion for nurturing both student achievement and teacher development.

Leading their respective departments with vision and dedication, they ensure that our curriculum remains cutting-edge and student-focused.

Through their leadership, our HODs create a collaborative environment where both students and educators are empowered to excel.

Their commitment to continuous improvement and personalized learning helps shape a future-ready generation.
